# Gotta Bag Them All

Welcome to **Gotta Bag Them All**, a ridiculous, over-the-top comedy game where the classic creature-collecting formula is flipped on its head. Instead of collecting monsters, you're collecting *people* — using nothing but paper bags and questionable ambition.

**Catchphrase:**  
> *"Gotta bag them all!"*

---

## 🎮 Overview

Step into a bizarre world filled with eccentric personalities, chaotic environments, and unpredictable encounters. As a self-proclaimed "Collector," your goal is simple: explore, strategize, and bag as many unique characters as possible.

From influencers livestreaming their every move to gym enthusiasts who refuse to skip leg day, every target brings a new comedic challenge.

---

## 🕹️ Core Gameplay

- **Bagging Mechanics**  
  Use timing, positioning, and strategy to successfully bag your targets.

- **Collection Album**  
  Track all the people you've collected, complete with humorous descriptions and traits.

- **Progression System**  
  Unlock new abilities, bag types, and techniques as targets become more difficult.

- **Bag Battles**  
  Pit your collected characters against each other in absurd, personality-driven encounters.

---

## 🌍 Game World

Explore a variety of stylized locations, each filled with unique characters:

- 🏖️ Beaches
- 🏙️ City Streets
- 🛍️ Shopping Malls
- 🎡 Themed Zones (because why not?)

Each area introduces new behaviors, challenges, and comedic situations.

---

## 🧠 Features

- Fully comedic, parody-driven tone  
- Dynamic AI reactions (running away, arguing, posing for selfies, etc.)  
- Wide variety of collectible personalities  
- Customizable paper bags with special effects:
  - Stealth Bags
  - Noisy Bags
  - Eco Bags (for style points)

---

- **Language:** C#  
- Designed with flexibility and scalability in mind  
- Built to support modular gameplay systems and AI behaviors

This game is intended as a **comedy parody** and should not be taken seriously.  
No real people are harmed, and all characters are fictional and exaggerated for humor.
